Polytope

Polytope

A class for general polytopes, and functions of general polytopes. Other than the construction property (which stores how the polytope was created), instances of this class doesn't contain any properties by default. These are set in the PolytopeC (combinatorial) and PolytopeS (symmetry) subclasses.
