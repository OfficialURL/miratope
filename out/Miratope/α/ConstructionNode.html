ConstructionNode

A ConstructionNode represents how a polytope has been built up. ConstructionNodes come in various types, and always have at least one child. Depending on the node type, these children can either be single objects, or arrays of ConstructionNodes or other objects.

A multiprism of multiprisms is just a larger multiprism, a multitegum of multitegums is just a larger multitegum, etc. This function removes children nodes of the same type and replaces them by their children.

getName()

Source:

Gets the name of a ConstructionNode based on its type. Recursively calls itself on the ConstructionNode's children when possible.

Example
//"great heptagram"
Polytope.regularPolygon(7,3).getName();

//"pentachoric prism"
Polytope.simplex(4).extrudeToPrism(1).getName();

//"pentagonal bipyramid"
Polytope.tegumProduct(Polytope.regularPolygon(5), Polytope.dyad()).getName();
