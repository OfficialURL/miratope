Class LinkedListNode<T>

A node, for usage in a (doubly) linked list. Each LinkedListNode is linked to two other nodes: linkage should be reflexive. LinkedListNodes don't necessarily have a notion of a "previous" and a "next" node. But when they do, node0 is the next node, and node1 is the previous.
